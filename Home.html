KuB (Kontakt- und Beratungsstelle für Flüchtlinge und Migrant_innen e.V. - english equivalent contact and helping point für refugees and migrants) is a non profit association based in Berlin-Kreuzberg which was established in 1983. Since that time we support refugees and migrants from all over the world. We do offer free counselling services on rigth of asylum and residence. Furthermore we do provide assistance in mental and social problems. We do believe that all people are entitled to a safe residence permit status as well as politcal, social and economical equality.
